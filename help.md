## About Bazaar Tracker

Bazaar Tracker is a local application that records your runs in **The Bazaar** and analyzes your performance.

All data is stored locally on your computer.

---

## Recording Runs

Runs can be added in several ways:

- Automatic log detection
- Screenshot OCR
- Manual entry

You can edit any run after recording it.

---

## Editing Runs

In edit mode you can:

- adjust hero or rank
- edit metrics
- change board items
- replace screenshots

Press **Save** to finalize changes.

---

## Perfect Runs

A perfect run is defined as:


10 wins and at least 25 prestige


Perfect runs are marked with **P** in the dashboard history.

---

## Achievements

Achievements are unlocked automatically based on your run history.

Examples include:

- winning with only small items
- cross-class item victories
- item collection challenges
- perfect run streaks

Achievements are rebuilt automatically from your run history.

---

## Templates and Images

Item templates are imported from the game’s `cards.json`.

Item images can be cached locally using the **Manage** page.

---

## Data Safety

Your data is stored locally in SQLite databases.

Use the **Manage → Export everything** feature to create backups.

---

## Troubleshooting

If something seems wrong, use:


Manage → Doctor


This tool checks the integrity of tracker data.
